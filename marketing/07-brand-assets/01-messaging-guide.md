## Tone of Voice

### Overall Tone
**Professional but approachable.** We're experts who don't talk down to users.

### Do:
- Use clear, simple language
- Be direct about what features do
- Acknowledge tradeoffs honestly
- Focus on outcomes, not just features
- Respect the reader's intelligence

### Don't:
- Use excessive jargon
- Overpromise or hype
- Pressure with artificial urgency
- Disparage competitors by name
- Use clickbait tactics

### Voice Characteristics

**Confident, not arrogant:**
- "WB Ad Manager handles all your monetization needs"
- Not: "WB Ad Manager is the best plugin ever made"

**Helpful, not pushy:**
- "If you need classifieds, Pro has you covered"
- Not: "You NEED to upgrade to Pro NOW"

**Honest, not defensive:**
- "The free version is complete for most use cases"
- Not: "Pro is totally worth it for everyone"

- "One-click" (for simple actions)
- "Native integration" (for BuddyPress/bbPress)
- "Unified dashboard" (for all-in-one benefit)
- "Passive income" (for classifieds/portal)
- "Self-service" (for advertiser portal)

### Outcome Phrases
- "Turn traffic into revenue"
- "Monetize without plugin bloat"
- "Let the system work for you"
- "Data-driven optimization"
- "Scale your ad business"

---

## Words and Phrases to Avoid

### Overused/Clich√©
- "Revolutionary" (we're not curing cancer)
- "Best-in-class" (everyone says this)
- "Cutting-edge" (meaningless)
- "Synergy" (corporate speak)

### Negative/Aggressive
- "Crush the competition"
- "Destroy" (anything)
- Competitor name-calling
- Fear-based urgency

### Misleading
- "Unlimited" (when there are practical limits)
- "Free forever" (unless it truly is)
- Guaranteed results (we can't guarantee revenue)
