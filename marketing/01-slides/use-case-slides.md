## Use Case 1: The Content Blogger

### Slide 1.1: Meet Sarah - Food Blogger

**Visual:** Food blog homepage

**Profile:**
- Runs a recipe blog with 50,000 monthly visitors
- Makes money from AdSense and affiliate links
- Spends too much time managing different plugins
- Wants more passive income without more work

**Pain Points:**
- 3 different plugins for ads, links, and popups
- No idea which ads actually perform
- Affiliate links scattered across 500 posts
- Email list growing too slowly

---

### Slide 1.2: How Sarah Uses WB Ad Manager

**Ad Setup:**
- AdSense in header (728x90 responsive)
- Sticky sidebar ad for her ebook
- Auto-inserted ads after paragraph 3
- Exit-intent popup for email capture

**Link Management:**
- All Amazon affiliate links cloaked
- Kitchen product links tracked
- Broken link detection saves commissions

**Results:**
- 40% increase in AdSense revenue (better placements)
- 3x more email signups (popup timing)
- Zero broken affiliate links
- 1 plugin instead of 3

---

### Slide 1.3: Sarah's Monthly Income Breakdown

| Revenue Source | Before | After |
|---------------|--------|-------|
| AdSense | $400 | $560 |
| Affiliate Sales | $200 | $350 |
| Ebook Sales | $50 | $150 |
| Email Value | $0 | $200 |
| **Total** | **$650** | **$1,260** |

**Key Insight:** Better ad placements and email capture nearly doubled income without more traffic.

## Use Case 3: The Community Site Owner

### Slide 3.1: Meet Mike - Fitness Community

**Visual:** BuddyPress fitness community

**Profile:**
- Runs a fitness community with 10,000 members
- Members post workouts, share progress, chat
- Wants to monetize without ruining experience
- Local gyms want to advertise

**Pain Points:**
- Doesn't want intrusive ads
- Members complain about popups
- Local businesses ask about advertising
- No way to sell ad space professionally

---

### Slide 3.2: How Mike Uses WB Ad Manager

**Native Ad Placements:**
- Ads in activity feed (every 6th activity)
- Sponsored posts look like content
- Sidebar widget for gym promotions
- Footer ads on forum pages

**Targeting:**
- Show gym ads only to local members (geo)
- Show supplement ads to workout posters
- Hide ads for premium members (user role)

**Advertiser Portal (PRO):**
- Local gyms create their own ads
- Prepay with wallet credits
- See their own analytics
- Mike just approves and collects

---

### Slide 3.3: Mike's Revenue Model

**Ad Pricing:**
- Activity feed ad: $100/month
- Sidebar placement: $75/month
- Forum sponsorship: $50/month
- Premium (ad-free) membership: $10/month

**Monthly Revenue:**
- 5 activity feed advertisers: $500
- 8 sidebar advertisers: $600
- 3 forum sponsors: $150
- 200 premium members: $2,000
- **Total: $3,250/month**

**Key Insight:** The ad revenue alone covers all hosting and development costs.
